What is Paperwork and why should I use it?

Simple job and client management

From the beginning, it was important that Paperwork scales up as your business grows. That's why we enforced a "job driven" workflow. All projects and tasks are Jobs. Jobs are assigned to a Client. That's pretty much it. This structure helps you juggle many jobs on the go, and allows you to see a client's job history.

"What you see is what you get" quote builder

See exactly what you're doing by building your quotes in-line. No going back and forward with little changes, what you see is what you get.

Autocomplete inventory items and prices

Paperwork stores the items you use along with their price so they can be re-used. When you begin adding an item into a quote, Paperwork will show suggestions. Bit of a no-brainer, right?

Bulk margin calculation

Store the item's cost price once, then apply a different margin for different circumstances. Add and remove margins as you see fit. Compare different margins. All without throwing your computer out the window.
