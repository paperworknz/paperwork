Core Features
Client Management Each Client gets their own page complete with important information, a notepad and a list of jobs. You can even collect your own data, too. Everything you need and nothing you don't.
Job Management A job is attached to a client and has a name and reference number. This is where you can easily build Quotes, Estimates, Invoices (or any type of form you like) and convert them to a PDF to send to your client. Jobs can be filtered by your own statuses and collect and update helpful data automatically.
Inventory Your inventory is a list of items and prices. This list turbo-charges quotes and invoices as it's available when adding items. If you're adding an item that isn't in your inventory you have the option to save it for future use - the more you use Paperwork, the easier it gets.
