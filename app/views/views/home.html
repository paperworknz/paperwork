Paperwork is a Quote and Invoice builder
Our mission is to make Quote and Invoicing as painless and stable as possible. We make purpose-built tools to order, simplify and minimise your paperwork - turning hours into minutes. We listen to our users and design tools and processes to get your Quotes and Invoices out the door as fast as possible.
Quotes and Invoices New Quotes and Invoices are made from your templates.
Paperwork provides a natural work flow, which means client details and job numbers are loaded automatically. Quotes are made inside Paperwork and can be converted to a PDF with the click of a button.
►
Inventory Quotes and Invoices are powered by Inventory.
Your inventory is a simple list of names and prices. There are many ways you can add to this list, including bulk upload from supported suppliers. (image)
Inventory in Action Just start typing an item name into a Quote and Paperwork will suggest items from your inventory. If you use a new material, and you'd like to use it again, you can add it to your inventory with a single tap.
►
And Much More We listen closely to our users and build anything that helps you get in and out as fast as possible. An example of this is the Margin interface. Tap the margin button to apply a margin to one, some, or all of the items in a Quote or Invoice.
